Welcome to Hanger Game!

Can you guess the words and save the Hangaroo? Try your luck in the Hanger Game arena!

Rules of the Game:

1. Click on the letters to guess the word.

2. You have three incorrect guesses before the game is over.

3. Use clues wisely to reveal letters in the word.

4. Earn points for correct guesses and completing words.

5. Have fun!
